## How to use
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.
3. To add your LinkedIn URL: open browser console and run:
   `localStorage.setItem('linkedin_url', 'https://www.linkedin.com/in/yourprofile')`
   Reload the page and your LinkedIn will appear.
